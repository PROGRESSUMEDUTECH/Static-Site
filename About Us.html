About Us

The Covid crisis had literally brought the world to a standstill at least for a brief period.According to the Forbes magazine around 22 million people had lost their jobs world over !
The aspirations of many a youngsters who are on the verge of completing their academics and waiting to plunge into their dream domains and usher as professionals have taken a backseat.
Three youngsters who have been in the skill development industry for the later part of the last decade took a brave to establish Progressum Edutec to bail the future professionals from any crisis in these hours of uncertainty.  We've a proven track record in leveraging skill based theories and practical methodologies to design,develop and deliver rewarding training programmes and integrate instructional technology to provide virtual,classroom and on the job training. We strive to empower and equip our wards with all the tools and techniques to tackle all the hurdles that may appear in the recruitment process and their professional fronts thereafter.
Consistent mock tests,sample interviews , regular reviews and constant efforts to stay in tune with the changing industry standards is what we provide for. We help them to identify their Achilles heel and refine them of their shortcomings. We thrive on the feedbacks that we receive from our wards in our surge ahead. We value them to the core to stay updated, genuine and true to our common cause.  Our land is not devoid of technically skilled professionals by any stretch of imagination but soft skills and aptitude is where we at times fall short. In addition to wholesome technically training we dream to impart and equip our coming generations with apt and able, soft skills and aptitude skills. We strive 24*7 to attain perfection.  We more share the sentiments of the students and entrepreneurs later.We plan on putting our experience in the skill development and placement industries to the best use so that we may help our younger brothers and sisters land their dream job. Progressum is your bridge that will connect you to your ambitions and aspirations.  Covid has coerced the world to go virtual, literally on all fronts. Even the education sector had to follow suit in the new normal era!  We and our determined team of trainers have developed the best in class training programmes and modules which will be a shot in the arm in the students preparations. Once the world is healed of all this suffering, we to will get back to normalcy.
